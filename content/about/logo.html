Our
Logo

We are happy for you to use our logo ether in full "FLXS" or in short-form "F" in your materials as long as the following guidelines are followed.

  • Use only our colours or a monochrome variation.
  • When using a monochrome variation, you can use any colour shade you wish but the entire logo must be in the same colour.
  • Leave clear space around the logo and do not overly distort it.
  • Generally, we're happy do let common sense prevail. If you get creative and it is still recognisable as our logo, we're happy with that.
